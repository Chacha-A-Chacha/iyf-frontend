Privacy Policy for International Youth Fellowship

Effective Date: Sunday 7 Apr, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to the International Youth Fellowship. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of the personal information we collect from our students. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information through our enrollment processes and participation in our courses.

Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information from our students:

  • Name
  • Date of Birth
  • ID Number
  • Name of Parent or Guardian
  • ID Number of Parent or Guardian
  • Current Residence
  • Course Selection

Additionally, we gather information on how you learned about our academy, which may include:

  • Flier
  • Social Media
  • Friend / Family
  • GNM Member
  • Continuing Student

How We Use Your Information

  • Manage course enrollments and maintain academic records.
  • Communicate with you about your courses and any changes to our services.
  • Provide support and respond to your inquiries.
  • Conduct statistical analyses to improve our educational offerings.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties. Your data may be shared with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as data management and email services. These providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements to ensure the security of your data.

Data Security

We implement various security measures to ensure the safety of your personal information both online and offline. These include encryption, access controls, and secure storage practices. Our website is also SSL-certified to enhance security.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information held by us. You may also opt out of certain uses of your information. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us at the details provided below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices. We will notify you of any significant changes and post the updated policy on our website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our use of your personal information, please contact us at Phone: 0708 333 444

Consent

Please read this policy carefully. By checking the box on our enrollment form, you confirm that you understand how we use your information and agree to the collection of your data for educational purposes.
